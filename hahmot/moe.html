Moe

Moammar Morris "Moe" Szyslak, also known as Moe the Bartender, is the proprietor and bartender of "Moe's Tavern", a Springfield bar frequented by Homer and his friends.
He has once stated that he moved to Springfield from the Netherlands.
He also owns Moe's Bagels in Colorado and Moe's Express in the Food Court area of the Springfield Mall.
Moe is portrayed as often irritable and rude to all except his bar friends Homer, Lenny, Carl, and Barney.

Moe is voiced by Hank Azaria.
