Apu Nahasapeemapetilon

Apu Nahasapeemapetilon, Jr., also known as Apu Du Beaumarchais,Slime Q. Slimedog and Steve Barnes, is the operator of the Kwik-E-Mart.
He is a recurring character in The Simpsons.
He is an immigrant from India, and as such, speaks with a strong Indian accent.

He was originally voiced by Hank Azaria.
